picture -- draws a Roche lobe, cvmovie style

picture draws a Roche lobe, cvmovie style.

Command invocation

picture q iangle phase r1 r2 rd roche delta ntheta1 nphi1 ntheta2 nphi2 rdisc1 rdisc2 height beta ndisct ndiscr nedge nplot spot device x1 x2 y1 y2 width reverse

Arguments

q---Mass ratio, q = M2/M1
iangle---Inclination angle, degrees
phase---The orbital phase to compute
r1---Radius of star 1, scaled by the binary separation
r2---Radius of star 2, scaled by the binary separation. The radius is measured along the line of centres towards star 1. If larger than the L1 radius, it will be set equal to it.
rd---Radius of the disc
roche---true/false to account for Roche distortion of secondary or not
delta---Accuracy in Roche lobe computations in terms of phase
ntheta1---Number of lines of equal latitude on star 1
nphi1---Number of lines of equal longitude on star 1
ntheta2---Number of lines of equal latitude on star 2
nphi2---Number of lines of equal longitude on star 2
rdisc1---Inner radius of disc
rdisc2---Outer radius of disc
height---Height of disc at r=1
beta---Flare exponent of disc
ndisct---Number of lines of theta on disc
ndiscr---Number of lines of radius on disc
nedge---Number of vertical lines at edge of disc
nplot---Number of points around equator of red star
spot---Bright-spot size (0 to suppress)
device---Plot device
x1---left-hand X limit
x2---right-hand X limit
y1---lower Y limit
y2---upper Y limit
width---plot width in inches
reverse---Reverse the asignment of black and white colour indices or not

Classes

This command is a member of the class: Model.
